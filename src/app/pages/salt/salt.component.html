SALT Score

AA severity in clinical trials based on the Severity of Alopecia Tool score.
SALT score is used in clinical trials to assess the extent of scalp hair loss.
Multiplying the percentage of hair loss in each of the 4 quadrants of the scalp by the quadrant surface area. Adding the 4 values together for a composite score. Enter in each quadrant the percentage of terminal hair loss from 0 to 100
Maximum SALT score of 100 indicates complete scalp hair loss Minimum SALT score of 0 indicates no hair loss
